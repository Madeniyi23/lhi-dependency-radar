# LHI Dependency Radar

**See what breaks before it breaks.**

---

## Before you delete a layer… do you know what will break?

Most ArcGIS Online environments grow fast:

- Web Maps  
- Dashboards  
- Experience Builder apps  
- StoryMaps  

Over time, dependencies become invisible.

So when someone:
- deletes a layer  
- renames a field  
- migrates a service  

👉 things break — silently.

LHI Dependency Radar is a Python tool for ArcGIS Online and ArcGIS Enterprise that helps GIS teams find where a hosted feature layer is being used before making risky changes.

---

## What this tool does

LHI Dependency Radar scans your ArcGIS environment and identifies where a hosted feature layer is used.

It helps you understand:
- which maps and apps depend on a layer  
- how it is referenced (item ID, service URL, layer URLs)  
- how many items may be impacted  

---

## Why it matters

This is not about GIS.

This is about:
- reducing risk  
- improving change confidence  
- enabling better governance  

---

## Who this is for

- GIS Analysts  
- GIS Administrators  
- Municipal GIS Teams  
- Anyone managing ArcGIS Online content
